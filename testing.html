TITLE


by USERNAME

--------

Taking a little break from Neopia to see its moon, but it's your first visit, and you're looking to fill out that itinerary? The Totally Real!: Guide to Kreludor! has your back. We've got you covered with a list of things to do, places to see, and even a couple to avoid at all costs. Let's launch right into it!

     

     

What's Good on Kreludor?

     

The View

     

     

The view of Neopia is spectacular from up here. Pick up a pre-packaged picnic basket from Cafe Kreludor or grab a book from Booktastic Books, then head out onto the surface. Kreludor has a breathable atmosphere, so don't worry about saving your breath!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: Kreludor doesn't have any weather, so the sky is always clear for stargazing!

     

Farside City

     

     

Kreludor is tidally locked, which means that, in general, the same side of the surface is always facing Neopia. Hidden on the side of the moon that can't be seen from Neopia is Farside City. Established after the Civil War, this city has become a sight to see. The defensive forcefield, which protects the city from frequent meteor showers, can be seen from afar and glows a brilliant purple with orange tinges as meteors burn up in the field!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: The far side is often referred to as the "dark side", but this is a misnomer. The farside sees daylight just as often as the near side!

     

The Kreludor Museum

     

     

Next to the Kreludor Spaceport is the Kreludor Museum. It sits here as a reminder of Kreludan history to all that come and go from the moon. The museum was built out of the remnants of a Civil War-era bunker and covers a variety of topics. The most popular exhibits are those related to the Civil War.

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: Admission is free, as the owners believe this is the best way to ensure that the mistakes of the past are not repeated.

     

The Nightlife

     

     

On Kreludor, one day cycle is equal to about 29.5 day cycles on Neopia. This means that Farside City gets just over 2 weeks of straight darkness at a time. They don't let that get them down, though, as there is always a party happening somewhere during the Kreludan night. So, if that's what you're looking for, schedule your trip accordingly!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: Most of these parties are still dedicated to the end of the Civil War and the defeat of Dr. Sloth.

     

Krelufun Industries

     

     

Also nestled on the far side of Kreludor, Krelufun Industries is where a lot of Neopia's plushies get produced. Kreludite from the various mines of Kreludor is used here to give the plushies their vibrant appearances. Want to get your hands on one? The new ownership now sells tours of the facility, during which you get to make your very own plushie!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: Patrons should be aware that a thief appears to frequently break into the facility, stealing plushies to resell on the black market. Hold your plushie tight!

     

The Farside Arcade

     

     

If you find yourself in Farside City, then a must-go is the Farside Arcade. This establishment is host to some of the most advanced gaming tech known to Neopia. If you've ever wanted to play Neoquest II in virtual reality, or you just want to Splat-a-Sloth, then this place is for you!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: The Farside Arcade rivals even that of Roo Island. Celebrity gamer AAA has been known to frequent this place, along with his younger sister Abigail.

     

Kreludor Spaceport Gift Shop

     

     

Before you head back to Neopia, remember to take a look at the gift shop located in the spaceport. This is your last chance to pick up some of the classics that you would normally find at Cafe Kreludor and Booktastic Books. You'll want to grab some postcards, and maybe even a rock or two!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: It is illegal to take rocks from the surface of Kreludor. Any rocks not purchased through the gift shop will be confiscated by security.

     

What's Bad on Kreludor?

     

The Kreludor Mines

     

     

If you can read the warning, then heed the warning! KEEP OUT!!! The mines of Kreludor are worked and patrolled by various robots, and they are programmed to exterminate anyone who shouldn't be snooping around! We can't all be Hannah, so steer clear!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: Some say that there is a secret route that extends from the near side mining facility all the way to Farside City.

     

The Meteor Fields

     

     

The far side is frequently inundated by meteor strikes, so don't plan your picnic here! It is highly advised to stay within the protected zones at all times. If you happen to find yourself outside of these zones, whatever you do, do not poke the meteors!

     

Totally Real! Krelduor Fact: Sentient rock-like creatures tend to prowl the far side during the Kreludan night, making outings even more dangerous!

     

The Neocola Machine

     

     

This machine is stationed on the near side of Kreludor. If you have a hankering for Neocola, it is advised that you find it somewhere else. While this machine has certainly been stocked with Neocola, it also seems to have been stocked with a variety of non-Neocola products. Some are even alive!

     

Totally Real! Kreludor Fact: If you still find yourself using the machine, beware of any non-Neocola beverages. Patrons have reported serious mutagenic side effects.

     

Travel Advisories

     

Prepare for the Surface

     

     

While Kreludor boasts a breathable atmosphere similar to Neopia's, it doesn't have similar gravity! At 34% of that of Neopia, you might find that you've got a bit more bounce in your step. A spacesuit is advised for those without their spacelegs, as unfamiliarity with the gravity can be disorienting and dangerous!

     

Be Careful who you Book With

     

     

Not everyone is licensed to fly you to the moon! When booking, make sure that you're doing so with a legitimate company. There are quite a few who will offer heavily discounted trips, at the cost of your comfort and safety!

     

Safe Travels!

     

     

We here at Totally Real! wish you all the best on your next trip to Kreludor. Remember to book through our travel agency to make doing these things and seeing these places even easier!
